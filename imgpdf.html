Frequently Asked Questions
Are my files uploaded to a server?
No. All conversions happen entirely inside your browser using JavaScript and the Canvas API. Your files are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored anywhere. Everything is 100% local and private.
How do I convert JPG to PNG?
Click the "Image Converter" tab → upload your JPG file(s) → select PNG as output format → click Convert Images → download your PNG file(s).
Can I convert multiple images at once?
Yes! In the Image Converter tab, you can upload multiple images at once and they will all be converted to the selected format. Use "Download All" to get them all at once.
How do I convert PDF to JPG?
Click the "PDF → Image" tab → upload your PDF → choose JPG as output → click Convert. Each PDF page will become a separate JPG image that you can download individually or all at once.
Which format is best for web — JPG, PNG, or WebP?
WebP is best for web — it's 25–35% smaller than JPG and 26% smaller than PNG with similar quality. Use PNG for logos/graphics needing transparency. Use JPG for photos where file size matters and transparency isn't needed.
Is there a file size limit?
Image conversion supports up to 20MB per file. PDF conversion supports up to 50MB. Since everything runs in your browser, very large files may be slower on older devices.
