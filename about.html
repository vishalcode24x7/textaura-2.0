What We Do

What is Textaura?

Textaura is a free online platform that provides a growing suite of text utility tools — from fancy Unicode text generators to QR code creation, symbol libraries, text decorators, and much more.

Everything on Textaura runs 100% in your browser, requires no account, and is completely free — forever. We believe useful digital tools should be accessible to everyone without barriers.

Our Mission

Why We Built Textaura

We noticed that many online tools, locked behind subscriptions, or require you to create an account just to do something simple. That's frustrating.

Textaura was built to be different — a clean, fast, and genuinely free toolkit that respects your time and privacy. No distractions, no data collection, just tools that work.
