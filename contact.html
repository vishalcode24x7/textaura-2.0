Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take to get a reply?
We usually reply within 24-48 hours on working days (Monday to Saturday). Emails sent on Sunday may take slightly longer. We try our best to respond to every message.
How can I suggest a new tool?
Email us at textaura24@gmail.com with the subject "Tool Suggestion" and describe the tool you'd like. We consider all suggestions seriously and add popular ones to our roadmap.
How do I report a bug?
Email us at textaura24@gmail.com with the subject "Bug Report". Please mention which tool/page had the issue, what happened, and your browser and device. We'll fix it as soon as possible.
Do you accept partnerships or collaborations?
Yes! We're open to tool collaborations, content partnerships, and co-marketing opportunities. Email us at textaura24@gmail.com with "Partnership" in the subject line.
Is Textaura free? Will it stay free?
Yes, Textaura is completely free and will always remain free. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no account required. That's our commitment.
