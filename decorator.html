Frequently Asked Questions

What is a text decorator?
A text decorator adds beautiful Unicode borders, frames, and decorations around your text. These are used for Instagram bios, WhatsApp statuses, Twitter profiles, and social media captions to make your text stand out.
How many decoration styles are available?
Textaura's Text Decorator offers 30+ decoration styles across 4 categories: Emoji Borders (stars, hearts, fire, crowns), Unicode Frames (brackets, neon, wave), Symbol Borders (arrows, diamonds), and Multiline Box styles.
Will decorated text paste correctly on Instagram?
Yes! All decorations use real Unicode characters — not images. They paste and display correctly in Instagram bios, captions, WhatsApp statuses, Twitter/X, and most text-accepting platforms.
Can I filter decorations by category?
Yes, use the category filter buttons (All / Emoji / Frames / Symbols / Multiline) to see only the decoration type you want. All cards update live as you type your text.
Why does my multiline decoration not look right?
Multiline decorations (like box borders) look best when viewed in a monospace font. They may appear slightly misaligned in some apps. Single-line decorations like "✦ Text ✦" work perfectly everywhere.
