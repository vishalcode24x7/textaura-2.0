Frequently Asked Questions

How do I copy a symbol?
Simply click on any symbol in the library. It will be instantly copied to your clipboard. A confirmation toast will appear at the bottom of the screen.
Will these symbols work on Instagram and WhatsApp?
Yes! All symbols are Unicode characters, not images. They paste and display correctly on Instagram bios, WhatsApp messages, Twitter, Facebook, Discord, and almost any text field.
How many symbols are available?
Textaura's Symbols Library contains 500+ Unicode symbols across multiple categories including Stars, Hearts, Arrows, Math, Currency, Music, Weather, Greek letters, and more.
Can I search for a specific symbol?
Yes, use the search box to find any symbol by name or keyword (e.g., "heart", "arrow", "star"). You can also filter symbols by category using the category tabs.
Why do some symbols not display correctly?
Some older devices or apps may not support all Unicode characters. Most modern smartphones and browsers display all symbols correctly. Try updating your OS or app if a symbol appears as a box or question mark.
