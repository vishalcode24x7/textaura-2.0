Frequently Asked Questions

How do I shorten a URL?
Paste any long URL into the input box and press Enter or click Shorten. Your short link will appear in seconds. You can copy it, share on WhatsApp, or open it directly from the result card.
Which URL shortening service do you use?
Textaura uses the TinyURL API to shorten links. TinyURL is one of the oldest and most reliable URL shorteners, with links that never expire. Your short link will always work.
Do shortened links expire?
No. TinyURL links are permanent and never expire. Your short link will continue to redirect to the original URL indefinitely — even years later.
Can I shorten any type of URL?
You can shorten any public URL that starts with http:// or https://. This includes website links, social media posts, YouTube videos, Google Drive files, and more. Private/local URLs cannot be shortened.
Is the URL shortener free?
Yes, completely free. No sign-up required. You can shorten unlimited URLs without any cost or restrictions.
