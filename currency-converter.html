Frequently Asked Questions
How accurate are the exchange rates?
Exchange rates are fetched from the open.er-api.com API, which aggregates rates from financial data providers. Rates are updated daily. For actual financial transactions, always verify with your bank.
How many currencies are supported?
Textaura's currency converter supports 150+ world currencies including USD, EUR, INR, GBP, JPY, AUD, CAD, CHF, CNY, AED, SAR, and many more. Use the dropdown to find any currency.
Is this currency converter free?
Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no hidden charges. Convert as many currencies as you need.
Can I convert Indian Rupees (INR)?
Yes! INR is fully supported. You can convert INR to USD, EUR, GBP, AED, SAR, and 145+ other currencies. Simply select INR in the "From" dropdown and choose your target currency.
How often are rates updated?
Exchange rates are updated daily. The rates reflect the previous business day's closing rates from global financial markets.
