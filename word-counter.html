Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word counter work?
Simply type or paste your text in the input box. Textaura instantly counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, unique words, reading time, speaking time, and shows top keywords — all in real time.
How is reading time calculated?
Reading time is calculated at 200 words per minute (average adult reading speed). Speaking time is at 130 words per minute (comfortable speaking pace). These are estimates and may vary per person.
What does "unique words" mean?
Unique words is the count of distinct words in your text. For example, if you write "the cat sat on the mat", there are 6 words but only 5 unique words ("the" appears twice).
What does the reading level mean?
Reading level is based on average word length. Easy = short, simple words (blog posts, social media). Medium = moderate complexity (articles, reports). Hard = long, technical words (academic papers, legal documents).
Is there a word limit for the tool?
No, there's no word limit. You can paste an entire article, essay, or book chapter. All processing happens in your browser so it's fast regardless of text length.
