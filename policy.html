Introduction

Welcome to Textaura ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit and use our website at textaura.com and all associated tools and pages.

By using Textaura, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at privacy@textaura.com.

✅

The short version: Textaura does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We have no accounts, no logins, and no data servers. Your text and interactions stay entirely on your device.

2

Information We Do NOT Collect

Textaura is designed to be completely private. We do not collect:

  • Any text you type, paste, or enter into any Textaura tool
  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • Your IP address or geographic location
  • Device information, browser type, or operating system details
  • Usage patterns, click behavior, or analytics data
  • Any form of behavioral or advertising profile data
3

Local Browser Storage

Textaura uses your browser's localStorage solely to remember your theme preference (dark or light mode). This data:

  • Stays only on your device — never sent to our servers
  • Contains only the value "dark" or "light" — nothing personal
  • Can be cleared anytime by clearing your browser storage

No cookies are used for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes.

4

Third-Party Services

Textaura uses a minimal number of external services:

  • Google Fonts — Used to load web fonts (Plus Jakarta Sans, Fira Code). Google may collect limited request data per their own privacy policy at policies.google.com.
  • QR Code API (goqr.me) — When you generate a QR code, the content you enter is sent to this public API solely to render the QR code image. Do not enter sensitive information (like passwords) in the QR generator.
  • jsDelivr CDN — Used to load the QR code generation library. Standard CDN request logs may apply per their policy.

We have no control over these third-party services' data practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for full details.

5

How We Use Your Information

ℹ️

We do not use your information because we do not collect any. All tool functionality — text conversion, symbol copying, QR generation, decoration — happens directly in your browser with no data leaving your device (except for the QR API noted above).

6

Advertising

Textaura does not display ads, does not use ad networks, and does not share any data with advertisers. The platform is completely free and ad-free — supported by our commitment to building useful tools without monetizing user data.

7

Children's Privacy

Textaura is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of inadvertently collecting data from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU under GDPR).

If you are a parent or guardian with concerns, please contact us at privacy@textaura.com.

8

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, most traditional privacy rights (access, deletion, portability) do not apply in the usual sense. However, you always have the right to:

  • Clear your browser's localStorage to remove the saved theme preference
  • Use Textaura in incognito/private mode for zero local storage
  • Contact us with any privacy questions or concerns
9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as we add new features or tools. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top.

We recommend checking this page periodically. Continued use of Textaura after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
